Welcome to my personal repository, where I document my **Reinforcement Learning (RL)** learning journey. I am open to suggestions or corrections from fellow learners!  
本仓库记录了我个人学习**强化学习 (RL)**的过程，欢迎各位提出宝贵意见和建议！

Ubuntu 22.04 | VS Code | Python

## Learning Resources | 学习资源

- **Main Reference 1**: [Mathematical Foundation of Reinforcement Learning](https://github.com/MathFoundationRL/Book-Mathematical-Foundation-of-Reinforcement-Learning), where I study reinforcement learning from the perspective of mathematical principles, and one of the difficulties lies in understanding the mathematical formulas.  
  **主要参考 1**：[强化学习的数学原理](https://github.com/MathFoundationRL/Book-Mathematical-Foundation-of-Reinforcement-Learning)，从数学原理角度学习强化学习,其中的难点在于对数学公式的理解。

- **Main Reference 2**: [Hands-on Reinforcement Learning](https://hrl.boyuai.com/), where I am learning reinforcement learning through Python code, and the difficulty lies in the dual assault of Python syntax and principles.  
  **主要参考 2**：[动手学强化学习](https://hrl.boyuai.com/)，通过Python代码学习强化学习,其中的难点在于Python语法和原理的双重围攻念。

## Completed Tasks | 已完成项

- **Mathematical Foundation of Reinforcement Learning**: I spent a considerable amount of time studying this book during the summer vacation. After the new semester began, I revisited the content. The biggest challenge was that even after completing the study, I struggled to apply the knowledge to practice, especially in integrating the robot’s state space and action space with the concepts. It wasn’t until I replicated a paper titled "[Humanoid-Gym](https://github.com/roboterax/humanoid-gym)" and examined its code line by line that I began to make progress. However, due to the complexity of the framework, I decided to start over and focus on "[Python 100 Examples](https://github.com/lingyu888/python_learning/) and Practical Reinforcement Learning."  
  **强化学习的数学原理**：在暑假期间，我花了相当长的时间才完成了这本书的学习。新学期开始后，我又复习了一遍相关内容。最大的问题在于，即便完成了学习，我也无法将所学知识应用到实际中，而且很难将机器人的状态空间和动作空间与之结合起来。直到我复现了一篇名为 “[Humanoid-Gym](https://github.com/roboterax/humanoid-gym)” 的论文，并逐行阅读了它的代码，我才逐步有所突破。然而，由于框架的复杂性，我选择从头开始，先从“[菜鸟一百题](https://github.com/lingyu888/python_learning/)和动手学强化学习”入手。
